Pre Nursery Program
Age Group 2.5 to 3.5 Years

Building on the foundation established in Play Group, our Pre-Nursery program takes your child on an exciting journey of exploration and growth, preparing them for a smooth transition to Kindergarten. Through fun activities, puzzles, and engaging co-curricular activities, children develop essential skills for future academic success:

  • Clear communication
  • Confident expression
  • Strong friendships
  • Refined movement and coordination
  • Gross and Fine motor skills
  • Early literacy & numeracy skills (including phonics awareness)
  • Reading Habit
  • Circle Time
  • Imaginative play
  • Independence in daily routines

This nurturing environment ignites a love for learning, transforming your child into a curious and confident explorer, ready to embrace the world!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age range for Pre-Nursery?

Our Pre-Nursery program is perfect for curious minds between 2.5 and 3.5 years old, building upon the foundation established in Play Group and preparing them for a smooth transition to Kindergarten.

How will Pre-Nursery help my child develop essential skills?

Through engaging activities and a play-based approach, children refine communication and social skills, develop independence in daily routines, explore early literacy & numeracy concepts, and strengthen coordination and creativity.

Does Pre-Nursery offer any extracurricular activities?

Yes! We understand the importance of well-rounded development. Our Pre-Nursery program offers engaging co-curricular activities like music, art, sports, gardening, and more to spark your child's creativity and explore various interests.

How will I be informed about my child's progress in Pre-Nursery?

We believe in open communication. Regular teacher-parent conferences and progress reports will keep you informed about your child's development and learning journey in Pre-Nursery.
