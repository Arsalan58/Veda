Playgroup Program
Age Group 1.8 to 2.5 Years

Our Playgroup program is a nurturing early learning program designed to ignite your child's curiosity and foster social-emotional development. Through a blend of engaging indoor & outdoor activities, circle time, and creative play, children develop essential preschool readiness skills like fine & gross motor skills, language fluency, social interaction, and cognitive abilities.

Frequently asked questions

What is the age range for Playgroup?

Our Playgroup program is designed for toddlers aged 1.5 to 2.5 years old, offering a safe and stimulating environment for their first steps in social learning.

What kind of activities are offered in the Playgroup?

Through a blend of free play, circle time, arts and crafts, and sensory exploration, our program fosters the development of fine & gross motor skills, language fluency, social interaction, and early literacy & numeracy concepts.

How will Playgroup benefit my child's social development?

Our program provides opportunities for toddlers to interact with peers, learn to share and take turns, and build foundational social skills that are essential for future success. It help in social interaction, communication skills, early reading habits and gross and fine motor skills. A toddler in a good playgroup environment is known to be cognitively sounder and more confident than a stay at home toddler. These toddlers develop a relationship with books, the ability to sit and focus.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio in Playgroup?

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio to ensure personalised attention and create a safe and nurturing environment for every child in our Playgroup program. In every batch of 15 - 20 pupils, we will have 2 teachers and a helper.
