About Us

Our Mission

At Veda, we're on a mission to redefine early education. As the largest preschool in the region, we're dedicated to providing a wholesome learning experience for every child with state of the art facilities. Our avant-garde approach emphasises hands-on learning in a digital and toxin-free environment, fostering curiosity and a love for exploration.

With a focus on instilling strong value systems and cultural appreciation, we celebrate diversity and honour each child's unique abilities. Shaping young minds with the sheer joy of learning is the cornerstone of our curriculum.

Playgroup Pre Nursery Parent & Toddler

Playgroup (1.8-2.5 Years)

Let your child blossom in our vibrant playgroup! Our program focuses on imparting motor, social, cognitive, and language skills, aiming to encourage curiosity and growth in every aspect of your child's development.

What to expect:

  • Develop essential skills: Learn to crawl, climb, jump, and refine fine motor skills like grasping and manipulating objects.
  • Build social connections: Make new friends, learn to share, and participate in group activities.
  • Spark curiosity and exploration: Explore their surroundings, ask questions, and solve problems independently.
  • Expand their vocabulary: Learn new words through songs, stories, and interactive play.

Pre Nursery (2.5-3.5 Years)

Get your child ready for kindergarten success with our engaging preschool program! We use a play-based approach to learning, fostering a love of exploration and discovery.

What to expect:

  • Language & Communication: Clear sentences, asking questions, early literacy skills.
  • Social & Emotional: Empathy, friendships, expressing emotions.
  • Fine Motor Skills: Utensil control, drawing shapes, tracing.
  • Gross Motor Skills: Balance, coordination, running, kicking/throwing a ball.
  • Cognitive Development: Colors, puzzles, problem-solving.
  • Early Literacy: Letters, sounds, print awareness.

Toddler and Caregiver Group (0-1.8 Years)

Bond with your little one and nurture their development through our Parent/Caregiver & Me group. This interactive program provides a supportive space for caregivers to learn alongside their babies and toddlers.

What to expect:

  • Engaging activities: Music, movement, sensory play, and more!
  • Expert guidance: Learn valuable strategies for early childhood development.
  • Social interaction: Connect with other parents and caregivers.
