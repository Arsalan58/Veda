Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the right age for my child to start at Veda?

18 months to 2 years is the right age for your child to start at Veda- the early learning preschool.

Q2. What is the admission procedure at VEDA - The Learning School?

The admission process at VEDA - The Learning School is based on a holistic review of each child. We encourage you to schedule an appointment for an enquiry and school tour to learn more about our program and meet our teachers. We look forward to getting to know you and your child!

Q3. What is the mode of payment to pay the fees?

We accept: Cheque Netbanking or Credit/Debit Card The fees are to be paid monthly and the preschool fees have to be paid for the full year. For more, please contact us at +91 99356 32350
